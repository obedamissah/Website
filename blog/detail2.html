Teamwork

Pair Programming

How did I feel before my first programming project? -NERVOUS because I did not have a teammate because nobody wanted to work with 'the amateur who might slow down progress'. It was a disheartening sight when I saw bright kids who also had no teammates and decided to work as individuals. They were really talented and their egos puffed up so that they would not want to even work with each other. Well, I was not alone. I found a CS greenhorn in the same class and decided to work with him. Some people abhor the idea of pair programming. And their thoughts sound like: 'I don't think that's going to work. What if I need to write code and my partner isn't available?' What if we disagree on the same way to solving a problem?' 'Creativity is relative and can hinder progress!' 'I can work alone; group work slows me down to zero.' Sounds reasonable, right? Yeah, but pair programming does more good than harm to upcoming programmers. Creativity may be relative but hinders progress only when both parties decide not to learn from each other. A yielding pair learn from each other's creative ideas -when one is preferred, the other is deferred for a suitable project other than being aborted. Consistent communication grows like-mindedness. It is said that great minds think alike; vice versa, like-minds are great and therefore produce excellent results in every task however simple it is. After couple of weeks, my partner and I got fond of each other and found ourselves at the zenith of the game. Such a remarkable phenomenon! I found myself in a real team which I had real conversations and entrusted work ethic. Pair programming taught me how to work in a team. Initially, I run into a personality clash with my partner -he was quite an introvert and even had hard times expressing his creative views. Sometimes he needed motivation; other times, he need pressure to work. All in all, I developed a great skill which will bolster me through my future career. Learning together was a great deal not only because it instilled in me work ethics such as teamwork skills and communication tactics, but also it revealed how easier and faster problems could be solved when great minds and determined hearts are pursue a task together.
